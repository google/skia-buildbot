Google Cloud Datastore
======================


Use Namespace to differentiate applications (Perf, Gold) and different
installations of the same application (Perf, Perf-Android, Gold, Gold-PDFium).

Naming convention for namespaces
--------------------------------

    <app>-<instance>
    <app>-localhost-<user>

Examples:

    perf
    perf-androidmaster
    perf-localhost-jcgregorio
    gold
    gold-pdfium
    gold-localhost-stephana
